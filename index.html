Welcome to the Star Trek: New Horizons Tech Tree!

An interactive tool to explore the technologies of the ST:NH mod.

  • Explore the Tree: Use filters, search, or simply pan and zoom to navigate the entire tech tree.
  • Branch Inspection: Click any technology to focus on its specific dependency branch. Use the new Back/Forward buttons to navigate your history.
  • Path Analysis: Right-click a technology to select it as a start point, and right-click another to set an end point. Then, render the direct path or all prerequisites in a focused popup view.
  • Share Your View: Use the "Copy Link" button to generate a shareable URL that saves your current filters, layout, and even a focused technology or calculated path.
  • Customizable UI: Choose from multiple layouts (including one with dependency arrows) and drag the control panel anywhere on the screen.
Beta / WIP:
This tool is still in development. Some features may not be fully functional.
Tip:
The more general your search or filters, the bigger (and slower) the tech tree becomes. Try to be specific for best results!
